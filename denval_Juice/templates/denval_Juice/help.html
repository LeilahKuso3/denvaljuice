How To Order

1. Click Order on the navbar to display the menu.

2. Click the juice you want to add to the cart.

3. To view your chosen items, click View Cart.

4. Click Submit Payment to complete your order.

How To Create Your Own Juice Blend

1. Go to Create Your Own Juice Blend.

2. Select the juices you want.

3. Click Create Blend to place your order.

4. Use the till number provided to make your payment.

5. Show the payment message at the counter to receive your order.

How to Pay

1. Add items to the cart.

2. Click View Cartto see your items.

3. Click Place Your Order Now to display the amount to be paid and the till number.

4. Go to Mpesa and use the till number provided to pay.

5. Show the payment message at the counter to receive your order.
